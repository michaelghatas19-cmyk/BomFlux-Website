Features

Everything your team needs to deliver

From first budget line to final handover, BomFlux gives you full visibility and control at every stage.

💰

Budget Intelligence

Track planned vs. actual spend in real time. Detect budget drift early with automated variance alerts and AI-powered burn-rate forecasting.

⚠️

Risk Register

Log, score, and monitor every risk with probability-impact matrices. Get automatic health score recalculation as risks evolve.

📦

Procurement Tracker

Keep tabs on every purchase order, supplier, and delivery. Tie procurement activity directly to budget lines and milestones.

📊

Financial Performance

Interactive dashboards show earned value, cost performance index, and forecast-to-complete — without the spreadsheet grind.

🧠

Foresight AI

Our built-in AI assistant answers questions about your project data, drafts weekly reports, and surfaces issues before they escalate.

📁

File Intelligence

Upload drawings, specs, and contracts. BomFlux extracts key data, links documents to tasks, and makes everything instantly searchable.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

No complex onboarding. No consultants required. Just sign up, import your project, and start tracking.

1

Create your project

Deliver with confidence

Weekly AI reports, live dashboards, and proactive alerts keep every stakeholder informed and every project on track.
